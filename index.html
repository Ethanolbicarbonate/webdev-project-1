eXperieNce aLl DifFerent KinDs of eMoTioNS

Here in the College of ICT, you will experience a vibrant array of emotions.

JOY

Ah, joy! That elusive emotion that you’ll experience as often as a blue moon in the night sky. It’s like finding someone who asked for your opinion. You’ll feel it when your code compiles without errors on the first try, or when you finally understand that complex algorithm that’s been giving you nightmares. It’s a fleeting moment of triumph, a rare gem in the rough terrain of ICT education. But don’t worry, these moments, however rare, are so intensely satisfying that they’ll fuel you through the more common emotions of frustration and despair.

FrUstrAtiOn

The classic “Why won’t this code work?” followed by the “Oh, I forgot a semicolon.” A rite of passage for every ICT student.
It’s the loyal companion that never leaves your side, always ready to greet you when your program crashes, or when the network lags in the middle of an important submission. It’s the bread and butter of the CICT experience, the secret ingredient that makes every success story worth telling. After all, what’s a good challenge without a healthy dose of frustration?

DesPaIR

Last but not least, we have despair. This is the emotion that creeps up on you in the quiet moments, making you question your life choices. Did you make the right decision choosing CICT? Is ICT really your calling? But remember, despair is just a sign that you’re pushing your boundaries and stepping out of your comfort zone. It’s a testament to your courage and determination. So, when you feel despair, take a deep breath, remind yourself of why you started this journey, and keep going. After all, the night is darkest just before the dawn.
